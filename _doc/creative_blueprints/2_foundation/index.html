Why PayFlow Exists

The freelancer payment gap

!
The Problem

Freelancers lose time and credibility every time they try to collect payment.

  • ▸No simple tool to send a payment request on the spot
  • ▸UPI payments arrive with no record beyond a bank SMS
  • ▸Chasing clients means manual follow-up with no status visibility
  • ▸Full invoicing suites are overkill for quick one-off project payments
✓
The Solution

Three screens. One button. Payment collected, result confirmed, record saved — in under a minute.

  • ▸Enter amount + service description → tap Pay
  • ▸Razorpay handles the full UPI checkout natively
  • ▸Success or failure confirmed instantly on ResultScreen
  • ▸Every transaction stored locally — no backend, no login
